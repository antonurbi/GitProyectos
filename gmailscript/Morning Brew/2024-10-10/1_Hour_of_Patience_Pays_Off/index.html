Hello from the top of the world! The summit of Cho Oyu!

Last but certainly not least, Andrew successfully reached the summit of Cho Oyu, the sixth-highest mountain in the world, alongside his Sherpa.


They had a rare and remarkable experience—being the highest people on Earth for about 30 minutes. At 8,200 meters, they were above the clouds, and during that time, no climbers were present on higher peaks like Everest or K2.

Astonishing sunrise at the summit of Cho Oyu - 6:30 AM

The summit conditions were cold and a bit windy, but otherwise perfect for a breathtaking sunrise. The light sweeping over the snow-capped peaks provided a serene and magical moment as they watched the world awaken below.


Andrew’s journey to the top started at 2:30 a.m., leaving from Camp 2 under a starry sky. By 6:30 a.m., they stood on the summit, greeted by the magnificent view that made every challenge and effort worth it.
